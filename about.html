About Me

Since graduating Humber College’s, Acting for Film and Television Program I have been, with great excitement, venturing out into the beautiful world of acting. Over the past year, I have continued to hone my craft as an actor, having had the blessings and incredible experiences of being both on stage and on-set.

I am so grateful to have had the experiences of being in three theatre productions, Barefoot in the Park (Scarborough Players), Sense & Sensibility (Amicus Productions) and A Midsummer Night’s Dream (A Company of Fools). Other credits that I have are, Chapparal, directed by Elizabeth Lazebnik (BravoFactual), Swerve, directed by Jason Armstrong (Skeleton Key Films Inc.) along with working alongside Michael Kennedy (credits include, Little Mosque on the Prairie) and Arthur Cooper (credits include, Shoot the Messenger and Room).
