About Me

As a recent graduate of Humber College’s, Acting for Film and Television Program I am incredibly excited to venture out into the beautiful world of acting. I have honed my craft as an actor, and know that my learning and exploring in this vast business, has not stopped just because I no longer walk through the Humber halls.

This is just the beginning!

I am so grateful to have had the experiences of being in two theatre productions, while training at Humber in both, Spoon River (written by, Edgar Lee Masters), directed by Maria Ricossa, as well as, The Woods (written by, David Mamet) directed by Dixie Seatle. Other credits that I have are, Beyond the Forest’s Edge, directed by Nicole Segal (Humber College), along with working alongside Michael Kennedy (credits include, Little Mosque on the Prairie) and Arthur Cooper (credits include, Shoot the Messenger and Room).
