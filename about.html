About Me

Since graduating Humber College’s, Acting for Film and Television Program I have been, with great excitement, venturing out into the beautiful world of acting. Over the past several years, I have continued to hone my craft as an actor, having had the blessings and incredible experiences of being on stage, on-set and in a recording studio.

I am so grateful to have had the experiences of working with great companies, such as, A Company of Fools and Shakespeare In Action. Select stage credits include A Midsummer Night's Dream & Twelfth Night (A Company of Fools), A Midsummer Night's Dream (Shakespeare In Action), and Hamlet(s) (Skipping Stones Theatre).

Voice-over credits include: Bubble’s Hotel and Hailey and the Hero Hearts, directed by Dee Shipley (Epic Story Media); Barbie Ride’N’Style and Fashion Fun for Everyone (Mattel); and Elinor Wonders Why (PBS).
